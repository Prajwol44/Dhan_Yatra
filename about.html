Our Story

Dhan Yatra was founded in 2023 by a team of financial enthusiasts and tech innovators who recognized a gap in Nepal's financial education landscape. We noticed that many Nepalis were interested in the stock market but lacked a safe, educational environment to learn and practice.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to democratize stock market education in Nepal. We wanted to create a platform where anyone, regardless of their financial background, could learn about trading without risking their hard-earned money.

Today, Dhan Yatra has grown into Nepal's premier fantasy stock trading platform, with thousands of users participating in our weekly competitions. We've helped countless Nepalis gain confidence in stock market investing while rewarding top performers with real cash prizes.

Our team combines expertise in finance, technology, and education to create an experience that's not just entertaining, but truly transformative for our users' financial futures.

Tournament Rules

Key rules to know before you start trading

Tournament Duration

Weekly tournaments run from Monday market open to Friday market close. All trades must be placed during market hours (11:00 AM - 3:00 PM NST).

Starting Capital

Each player receives ₹100,000 in virtual currency to build their portfolio. You can invest in any stock listed on the Nepal Stock Exchange (NEPSE).

Trading Rules

You can buy/sell stocks anytime during market hours. A 0.5% brokerage fee is applied to each transaction. Maximum 20 trades per day.

Portfolio Value

Your portfolio value is calculated using closing prices each day. Final ranking is based on Friday's closing value.

Private Tournaments

Create private tournaments with friends! Set custom entry fees and prize distributions. Minimum 5 players required.

Bonus Cash

Earn bonus cash through daily login rewards, achievement milestones, and by referring friends. Bonus cash can be used for entry fees.

Scoring System

How your portfolio performance is calculated

Component Description Points Calculation
Portfolio Value Total value of all your holdings Closing price × Quantity held
Daily Returns Percentage change in portfolio value (Today's value - Yesterday's value) ÷ Yesterday's value
Consistency Bonus Reward for positive daily returns +10 points for each day with positive returns
Diversification Holding stocks from multiple sectors +5 points per unique sector (max 25 points)
Transaction Fees Cost of buying/selling stocks 0.5% of transaction value deducted

Final Score Calculation

Your final score = Portfolio Value + (Daily Returns × 1000) + Consistency Bonus + Diversification Bonus

Example: A portfolio worth ₹115,000 with 3 days of positive returns and stocks from 4 sectors would score:

115,000 + (15% × 1000 = 150) + (3 × 10 = 30) + (4 × 5 = 20) = 115,200 points

Pro Trading Tips

Strategies to improve your chances of winning

Research is Key

Study company fundamentals, sector performance, and market news. Our app provides detailed company profiles and financials.

Diversify Your Portfolio

Spread investments across different sectors (banking, insurance, hydropower, etc.) to minimize risk and earn bonus points.

Focus on Blue-Chips

Consider stable, high-performing stocks like NABIL, NICA, and SCB for a solid foundation in your portfolio.

Monitor Market Trends

Pay attention to market indices, trading volumes, and sector rotations to spot emerging opportunities.

Time Your Trades

Market openings and closings often have higher volatility. Place strategic trades during these periods.

Learn from Top Players

Follow expert traders in our community. Analyze their strategies and portfolio allocations.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To democratize stock market education through an engaging, risk-free gaming experience. We believe everyone deserves the opportunity to learn investing principles without financial barriers.

By combining real market dynamics with fantasy gaming mechanics, we've created a platform that transforms complex financial concepts into an accessible, enjoyable experience.

Our Vision

To become Nepal's leading platform for financial literacy, empowering a new generation of informed investors. We envision a world where financial knowledge is accessible to all, regardless of background or experience.

Through DhanYatra, we're building a community of financially savvy individuals who can make better investment decisions in their personal lives.
